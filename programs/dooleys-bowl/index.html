DOOLEY'S BOWL

RHA Presidents may come, RHA Presidents may go; students may come, students may go; your GPA may come, your GPA may go; but Dooley goes on forever!

what is dooley's bowl?

Dooley's Bowl is a competition between first-year Residence Halls. You know, because Emory students need to compete more. The format is pretty simple: the hall with the most points at the end of the year wins (and gets a prize). The competition is split between the two semesters, with one hall being crowned Dooley's Bowl Semester Champions, and the hall with the most cumulative points at the end of the school year is the official Dooley's Bowl Champions! The winning hall will have a party in their residence hall thrown by the other halls.
